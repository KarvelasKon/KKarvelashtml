A JOURNEY OF A THOUSAND MILES BEGINS WITH A SINGLE STEP...

As a reference flights are a strange destination from country to country because all flights go through the capital of Vietnam so they have a long delay. However, inside the countries things are a bit better as you can use the bus or train for very little cost. After some research I think the best way is to start in Laos and end in Vietnam.

Singapore--Luang Prabang

Luang Prabang--Vientiane

Vientiane--Pakse

Pakse--Krong Siem Reap

Krong Siem Reap--Krong Battambang

Krong Siem Reap--Phnom Penh

Phnom Penh--Ho Chi Minh City

Ho Chi Minh City --- Hanoi

Hanoi--Singapore
